Welcome to SOLARIS

Solar energy is the future. To use solar energy people must first understand how it works and what it can do with it. To buy and install a solar system is an investment for the future. Solar systems today are available for everyone to buy, they are cheaper than ever and very efficient. A solar system can last up to 25 years before any service or repair is needed. It is a good investment for rural areas where power is not available or urban areas where in combination with the electricity grid can create a lot cheaper home to live in and also an eco-friendly solution for big cities that require a lot of power to maintain.
