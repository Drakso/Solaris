Photovoltaic System solution

The photovoltaic system converts solar energy to electricity for powering all sorts of appliances. These systems can be Off-Grid and On-Grid. Off-grid systems convert solar power and store it in a battery for later use. This systems are usually used in rural areas where there is no power, areas where power installation is very expensive ( as much as a whole solar system ) or for people that want an independent source of energy for their house. On-Grid systems are same as Off-Grid but the building does not relly solely on the power the solar system provides. This system usually don’t include battery but it can be additionally added. In some countries when the power of the photovoltaic system is not in use, it goes back in the power grid and the owner gets paid for the power that has been generated. More Info

Boiler System Solution

Solar systems with boiler are the most common on the residential market. These systems are used for heating water. The water is heated in a boiler which can be attached on the panel, on the roof or inside of the building. Throughout the years, the indoor boilers have proven to be more efficient. More Info

Heating System Solution

Solar systems can be used for heating too. These systems also use an indoor boiler but this boiler is bigger and built for heating purposes. The boiler is connected to the house radiator. In winter we don’t usually see the sun but that doesn’t mean that it is not radiating solar energy. More Info
